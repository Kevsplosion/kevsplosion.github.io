About me: kevin aslan

Born in 1995 and raised in Oslo, I was quick to decide that game development was my future coming into high school. I have a burning passion for creating experiences for people through games. Fluent in both Norwegian and English, with a little bit of Turkish on the side, I rarely have problems communicating both locally and internationally.

I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in IT specializing in game design from Westerdals - Oslo School of Art, Communication and Technology in the summer of 2017.

I’ve had major experience in the following tools and software:

  • Unreal Engine
  • Blender
  • Game Maker


Intermediate experience in:

  • Substance Painter
  • VR (Virtual Reality)
  • Blueprints (Unreal Engine’s visual scripting language)
  • Perforce
  • Jira
  • Adobe Premiere
  • Adobe After Effects


And some experience in:
